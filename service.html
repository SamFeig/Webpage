2. A brief description of your community service activity and how it went. Why did you choose this activity? Who did you impact? Include any comments you received during or after the activity from the participants.
In this activity I was giving feedback to the Buff Portal UI/UX team about my experience with it and my thoughts and suggestions about how to improve Buff Portal. I chose this because my original activity got canceled due to Corona Virus and Chris suggested it as an online alternative. I helped impact the team developing Buff Portal and hopefully everyone who will be using Buff Portal in the future by making it better for all of us. One notable comment I recienved was that they thought it was a great idea to to have a customizable home page that you could add panels you use frequently

3. How did you feel before and after the activity? Did you make any connections? Do you have plans to do future community service?
Before this activity I thought it would be a bit of a waste of time. I had given feedback through their feedback box before but never seen anything come out of it. After this event I felt like my feedback and issues were actively heard and have a greater potential to be implemented and fixed. I have the contact information for the Buff Portal team now so if I have further ideas, I can send them directly to a person. I do have plans to do future community service since I enjoy helping organizations that have helped me in the past. Once we are no longer under quarantine, I had already planned to help out the original organization (FIRST) that I had planned to before their event was canceled.
